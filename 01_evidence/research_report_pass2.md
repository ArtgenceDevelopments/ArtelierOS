### Gap #5 — French-language buyer voice (was: thin)
**Status: closed, and it surfaced the most valuable single finding of the whole project.**
- **E-035**: French pricing anxiety is identical to anglophone, more emotionally framed.

- **E-040**: a French emerging-artist resource runs a WhatsApp channel, corroborating the WhatsApp instinct.

## The single most important consequence
The French e-invoicing reform converts Artelier's hardest problem (technology-adoption friction) into a state-mandated tailwind — **but only if Artelier emits compliant structured invoices through an approved platform.** Right now it emits PDFs, which the reform disqualifies. This is now an architecture and legal decision, not a marketing line. It belongs in the pending expert-comptable/avocat consult before any "compliant French invoicing" claim ships.
