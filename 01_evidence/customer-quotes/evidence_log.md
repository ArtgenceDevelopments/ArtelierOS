### Evidence #E-001
**Name:** "everything in one platform"
**Evidence Type:** quote
**Source Type:** Public Communities
**Evidence Format:** quote
**Link:** https://www.capterra.com/p/169087/Artwork-Archive/reviews/
**Source Date:** 2026-03-13
**Theme Buckets:** desire | delight
**Journey Phase:** use
**Confidence:** medium

> "everything in one platform and keep an accurate inventory" — Artwork Archive user, 20+ years of work catalogued.

**Reveals:** Buyers frame the payoff as *consolidation + accuracy across decades*, not features. The emotional win is "I can finally see my whole body of work in one place." Artelier's "one app for your entire art business" lands on this nerve — but because Artwork Archive already owns the all-in-one-inventory claim, the differentiator must be what it lacks (deterministic pricing + sale workflow), not the consolidation itself.
