## Evidence rules of engagement (already operating — ratify as binding)

These governed the research pass and should become standing decision rights:

1. **No source URL, no entry.** Every evidence item carries a live link and capture date. _(research_report.md standard)_
2. **Unrecorded conversation is not evidence.** "We know from sales calls" with no linkable artifact cannot be logged — this is why `internal-proof/` is an honest GAP, not a filled folder. _(internal-proof/evidence_log.md)_
3. **No fabrication, fragments verbatim.** Copyrighted reviews are captured as the distinctive fragment + link, never reproduced whole; no screenshots are invented. _(research_brief.md method notes)_
4. **Scope correction beats scope drift.** Researching the wrong audience poisons every downstream pattern; a corrected scope is logged, not quietly patched. _(research_report.md Phase 0)_
